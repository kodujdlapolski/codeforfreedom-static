    Project pitches – format
    Lets make the pitches following this formula: 1) Project name & logo/image 2) Mission - how your project supports freedom/ democracy /solidarity? 3 & 4) What is it? - target group and main functionalities of the app 5) What you plan to do at the hackathon & what kind of help do you need? Coding or discussing concept/functionality/promotion? max. 5 minutes for everyone max. 5 slides Please, send your presentation in PDF to karolina.wysocka@epf.org.pl & krzysztof.madejski@epf.org.pl by Friday evening.   And for the final presentations on Sunday: Title & logo Content -what you have :) Where’s social impact? Next plans & how would you spend the hackathon award? Team - all people and one contact.
