Localisation

It’s in Gdańsk, the city of freedom, where the ‘Solidarity’ movement started in the Lenin Shipyard. Check the place:

 

 

And it has a cool president who understands technology & openness, check his video message.

 

We will work in the Shipyard, in the new European Solidarity Center – you can do free sightseeing there!
It’s next to the huge ‘Monument to the Fallen Shipyard Workers of 1970′. Do not trust google map, we know better that it’s just 20 meters behind those monumental crosses & the historical Shipyard gate. You cant see it in street view because the building is so new. Official address: Doki 1, but just ask anyone about the ‘huge crosses at Shipyard’ and you will get there! It’s just walking distance from the Bus/Train Station and the old town

 

 

Participants stay in Hotel Amber & Hostel Grand.
